Air-Fried Hard-Cooked Eggs

I love not having to wait for the water to boil and not having to wash the pot. I just place my eggs in the air fryer and in 15 minutes they're hard-boiled.

Ingredients

  • 6 egg whites
  • Bowl of cold (or iced) water

Steps

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C)
  2. Place eggs in the air fryer basket.
  3. Cook eggs for 15 minutes. Remove eggs and place in a cold water bath until fully cooled, 8 to 10 minutes
